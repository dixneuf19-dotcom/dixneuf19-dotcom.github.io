About Me

Hi! I’m Elise Chau, a secondary educator teaching Chinese, French and Information Technology. I believe education is a shared journey: each classroom is a space where curiosity and respect meet, and where every student’s voice shapes collective learning. Drawing on Confucian values of mutual learning and respect, I encourage students to both teach and learn from one another — because growth is most powerful when it’s reciprocal.
